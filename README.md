# CCAT Test Harness

Welcome to the **Credential Cognitive Ability Test (CCAT)** administered through Claude.

---

## What is the CCAT?

The CCAT is a 50-question cognitive ability test that measures your:
- **Verbal Reasoning** — Understanding language and logic
- **Quantitative Reasoning** — Math and numerical problem-solving
- **Spatial Reasoning** — Visualizing objects and patterns

The test is designed to assess your problem-solving ability and cognitive skills in a fair, objective way.

---

## How to Take the Test

### Before You Start

- **Find a quiet place** with minimal distractions
- **Have paper and pen** nearby if you'd like to work out problems
- **Plan for about 15 minutes** (soft time limit; test completes when you finish)
- **Be ready to focus** — once you start, you'll move through questions sequentially

### Starting the Test

Simply tell me: **`start`**

I will:
1. Ask a few clarifying questions to ensure you're ready
2. Explain how the test works
3. Present the first question

### During the Test

For each question:
1. **Read carefully** — Take time to understand what's being asked
2. **Provide your answer** — Type your response clearly
3. **Move forward** — I'll present the next question (no going back)
4. **Continue** — We'll move through all 50 questions in order

**Important:** You cannot skip questions or return to previous ones. Answer each question as best as you can, then move on.

### Time Expectations

- **Typical duration:** 12-16 minutes
- **If you exceed 30 minutes:** I'll check in to make sure you're okay and want to continue
- **You can always stop:** If you need to exit before finishing, let me know

---

## What Happens After You Finish

Once you've answered all 50 questions, I will:

1. **Score your test** — Calculate your final percentage
2. **Break down results by domain** — Show you how you performed in:
   - Verbal Reasoning
   - Quantitative Reasoning
   - Spatial Reasoning
3. **Provide analysis** — Explain your strengths and areas for growth
4. **Give a recommendation** — One of three outcomes:
   - **Hire** — You performed well
   - **Further Assessment** — Results suggest additional evaluation may be helpful
   - **No Hire** — Performance did not meet the threshold

## Common Questions

**Q: Is there a time limit for each question?**  
A: No. Take the time you need on each question. The overall test is soft-timed at 15 minutes, but will complete whenever you finish.

**Q: Can I skip a question and come back to it later?**  
A: No. Questions are presented in order, and you must answer each one before moving to the next.

**Q: What if I don't know the answer to a question?**  
A: Make your best guess or give it your honest attempt. There's no penalty for trying.

**Q: Can I use a calculator?**  
A: Yes, if you want to verify math. The test is assessing your reasoning, not your ability to do calculations by hand.

**Q: Will my answers be compared to the "correct" answers?**  
A: Yes, but only after you've finished all 50 questions. You won't see the answer key during the test.

**Q: What happens if my connection drops mid-test?**  
A: Don't worry. The test can recover from interruptions. We'll pick up where you left off and continue from the next question.

**Q: How is the final score calculated?**  
A: Your score is the percentage of questions you answer correctly: `(Correct Answers / 50) × 100%`
